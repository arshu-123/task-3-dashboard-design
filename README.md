# task-3-dashboard-design
Walmart Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard

## Project Title
Walmart Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard

## Objective
The objective of this project is to design an interactive dashboard that enables business stakeholders to monitor sales performance, analyze trends, and support data-driven decision making.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Microsoft PowerPoint
- Walmart Sales Dataset

## Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards
- Total Sales
- Total COGS
- Total Gross Income
- Total Quantity Sold

### Interactive Filters
- City
- Branch
- Gender

### Visualizations
1. Product Line Sales Breakdown by City
2. Sales Performance by Time of Day
3. Monthly Sales Trend
4. Sales and Quantity Performance by Product Line
5. Gross Income by Customer Type
6. Sales Performance by City and Product Line

## Key Insights

- Total Sales reached 320.89K.
- Naypyitaw generated the highest sales.
- Evening sales performed better than morning sales.
- Member customers generated higher gross income.
- Food and Beverages and Fashion Accessories contributed significantly to sales.

## Outcome

Developed an interactive dashboard that provides a comprehensive view of sales performance and helps stakeholders identify trends and business opportunities.
